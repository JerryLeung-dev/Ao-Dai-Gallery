The Young Girl with Lily

The Young Girl with Lily is considered to be the most famous work of artist To Ngoc Van as well as one of the most representative representatives of Vietnamese Fine Arts in the 20th century. The painting of a girl with lilies was created by artist To Ngoc Van in 1943. The picture depicts a portrait of a young woman in a white ao dai next to a vase of white lilies. Tilting his head down to fragrant flowers, shirt colors and flowers blended together to create a gentle color, a bit of sadness and lingeringness. The model of the painting is the artist's Six Girl, She is also a model for many other works including the Lotus Girl. Besides To Ngoc Van, Ms. Sau is also a model for many famous contemporary painters such as Tan Van Can, Nguyen Gia Tri and Luong Xuan Nhi. Young girl with lilies Later sold to famous collector Duc Minh (real name Bui Dinh Than). After his death, this work was sold to his collector by a son named Ha Thuc Can for $ 15,000 and Mr. Can sold this famous work abroad.

Autumn Noon

This is a beautiful painting portraying the falling leaves of autumn covering the street corner one autumn noon. The master landscape painter of Asia Lam Manh visually presents the delicate poetry autumn season has. The lavish coloring and vibrant painting surface remind us of its transient beauty and prompts to explore it deeply. The artwork is signed on the back and on the front.
